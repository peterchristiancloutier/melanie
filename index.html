Welcome to The Wishing Tree, a joyful collection of my handmade jewelry, where each piece is crafted with love, imagination, and a touch of magic. I started The Wishing Tree as a way to share my wish: that every piece brings a little joy to its wearer, blending organic simplicity with decadent detail. My pieces are designed to make women all ages feel radiant, uniquely themselves, and always a little bit enchanted. You deserve to know wishes come true!

I was raised in Belmont, California and started The Wishing Tree while attending Santa Clara University.
